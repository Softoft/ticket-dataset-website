About Dataset

The Email Ticket Text German Classification dataset provides a comprehensive collection of email tickets written in German, classified by department and priority level. This dataset is designed for natural language processing, machine learning, and customer support optimization. It includes emails categorized into three departments: Software, Hardware, and Buchhaltung (Accounting), with priorities ranging from 1 (low) to 3 (critical).

For access to the full dataset, please email me at dataset@softoft.de. This is only a preview. This preview has 200 randomly selected rows from the real dataset which has 8k+ rows.

Key Features

  • Email Texts: Authentic email content in German, providing real-world scenarios for text analysis.
  • Department Classification (Queue): Emails are classified into three main categories:
    • Software: Issues related to software applications and systems.
    • Hardware: Issues related to physical devices and hardware components.
    • Buchhaltung: Accounting and financial-related inquiries.
  • Priority Levels: Each email is assigned a priority level:
    • 1 (Low): Non-urgent issues.
    • 2 (Medium): Moderately urgent issues.
    • 3 (Critical): Urgent issues that require immediate attention.

Use Cases

  • Text Classification: Train machine learning models to classify email content into appropriate departments.
  • Priority Prediction: Develop algorithms to predict the urgency of emails for efficient ticket handling.
  • Customer Support Analysis: Analyze and improve customer support processes by understanding the nature and urgency of issues.
